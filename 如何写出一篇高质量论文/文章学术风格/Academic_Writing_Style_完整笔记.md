## Part 1 — Sentence Length, Conciseness, Clarity and Ambiguity

### 1.1 每句最多两个信息（maximum two ideas per sentence）

- 理想情况下，每句只包含 **一个信息（one piece of information）**，且 **不超过约25个词**。
- 偶尔出现的长句是可以的，前提是：(1) 易于理解；(2) 拆分成短句反而更难。

| ✅ YES                                                                                                                                                                                                     | ❌ NO                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| The majority of words recorded in a modern English dictionary have been borrowed from other languages. However, the words ordinarily used in speaking are largely of English origin.*(16, 13, 13 words)* | In the language as recorded in a modern English dictionary the great majority of words are borrowed; but the words we ordinarily use in speaking are largely of English origin...*(45 words)* |

---

### 1.2 按时间顺序写（put information in chronological order）

> *Your document should be like a map showing the reader the direction to follow. Try to write in a step-by-step way, with each step logically following the previous one.*

- **a.** 在句子的第二部分，优先用 **then + 简单过去时**，而非 *after + 过去完成时*。
- **b.** 用 **first(ly), second(ly)** 等词来标示步骤顺序。

| ✅ YES                                                                                            | ❌ NO                                                                                                      |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| The vegetables were**cooked** in the oven and **then served** with the main course.   | The vegetables were**served** with the main course **after they had been cooked** in the oven. |
| When defusing a bomb,**first** disrupt the circuit. **Secondly**, cut the green wire. | When defusing a bomb, cut the green wire**after first having disrupted** the circuit.                |

💡 **类比理解：** 按时间顺序写作就像在给人讲乐高拼装步骤——你不会在说"插入B块"之前才提"先找到A块"，顺序乱了，读者就迷路了。

---

### 1.3 避免插入语（avoid parenthetical phrases）

- **a.** 重新排列句子，让**主语和动词紧挨在一起**。句子顺序取决于你想强调的重点。
- **b.** 当插入信息较长时，**拆成两句话**。

| ✅ YES                                                                                                                               | ❌ AVOID                                                                                                                        |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| The**vegetables were cooked** in the oven **and then** served with the main course.                                      | The**vegetables**, which had been cooked in the oven, **were served** with the main course.                         |
| **We believe** the results are significant given their innovative nature. **When** they are analysed they should help... | The analysis of the results,**which we believe are of a significant value given their innovative nature**, should help... |

---

### 1.4 简洁用词（be concise）

用更简洁的词替代冗长表达：

| ✅ YES                                                                     | ❌ AVOID                                                                                  |
| -------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| We believe the results are**significant**.                           | We believe the results are **of significant value**.                               |
| They should be**green and round**.                                   | They should be**green in color and round in shape**.                                |
| **Note** that the sum of the values needs to be lower than…         | **It is worth noting / Bear in mind** that the sum of the values…                  |
| We found that x = y.**Under** certain circumstances x also equals z. | We found that x = y.**In particular**, under certain circumstances x also equals z. |

---

### 1.5 优先使用动词而非名词（prefer verbs to nouns）

- **a.** 用**动词**代替名词，提升可读性和简洁度。
- **b.** 用**一个动词**代替"名词 + 动词"结构。

| ✅ YES                                                              | ❌ AVOID                                                                       |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| This was used to**calculate** the values.                     | This was used**in the calculation of** the values.                       |
| The USA**was compared** to the Russian Federation.            | **A comparison was made** between the USA and the Russian Federation.    |
| The Russian Federation**performed** much better than the USA. | The Russian Federation**showed a much better performance** than the USA. |

💡 **类比理解：** 用动词而非名词，就像直接说"我跑了5公里"而不是"我完成了一次5公里的跑步行动"——动词更有力，读者感受到的是动作本身，而不是对动作的描述。

---

### 1.6 优先使用形容词而非名词（use adjectives rather than nouns）

- **a.** 用 **动词 + 形容词** 结构，而非 *动词 + 名词*。
- **b.** 用 **more + 形容词**，而非 *比较级形容词 + 名词*。

| ✅ YES                                                           | ❌ AVOID                                                                       |
| ---------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| This method has quite an**efficient** calculation process. | This method shows quite a good**efficiency** in the calculation process. |
| X is**more homogeneous** than Y.                           | X has**a higher homogeneity** with respect to Y.                         |

---

### 1.7 小心人称代词（be careful of use of personal pronouns）

- **a.** 通过将主语**复数化**，用 *they / their / them / theirs* 来避免 *he / she*。
- **b.** 当主语必须是单数时，不要只用阳性代词，改用 *he / she, him / her, his / her*。

| ✅ YES                                                                                            | ❌ AVOID                                                                              |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| If**traders** are trading on several markets and **they** wish to…                   | If**a trader** is trading on several markets and **he** wishes to…       |
| If Trader A wants to send**his / her** order to the market then **he / she** has to… | If Trader A wants to send**his** order to the market then **he** has to… |

---

### 1.8 we / us / our 的必要与非必要用法

*we* 的使用有时是必要的（避免歧义），但在以下情况可以避免：

- **a.** 不用 *we* 可以更简洁时。
- **b.** 几乎每句都在用 *we* 时——改用**被动语态或动名词**。

| ✅ YES                                                                         | ❌ AVOID                                                                   |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| This document**outlines** the main points of xyz.                        | **In this document we outline** the main points of xyz.              |
| The discussion of integers**is now continued** to extend the notion of… | **We continue our** discussion of integers to extend the notion of… |
| **Before dealing** with this issue…                                     | Before**we deal** with this issue…                                  |

---

### 1.9 避免口语词和缩写（avoid informal words and contractions）

- **a.** 举例时用 **such as** 代替 *like*。
- **b.** 因果关系用 **thus / therefore / consequently** 代替 *so*；用 **in any case** 代替 *anyway*。
- **c.** 对比关系用 **however / nevertheless / yet** 代替 *but*（放句首时）。
- **d.** 过渡用 **moreover / furthermore / in addition** 代替 *also*。
- **e.** 避免缩写形式（如 *it isn't, we'll*），改用完整形式（*it is not, we will*）。
- **f.** 避免口语化短语动词（如 *check out, get around, give up, work out*）。

| ✅ YES                                                                     | ❌ CONSIDERED TOO INFORMAL                                           |
| -------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| A few European countries,**such as** Montenegro...                   | A few European countries,**like** Montenegro...                |
| We**thus** had to… **In any case**, this was useful because… | **So** we had to… **Anyway**, this was useful because… |
| Clinton's argument does not appear to**make sense**...               | Clinton's argument does not appear to**add up**...             |

---

### 1.10 强调用 do / does（emphatic do / does）

- **a.** *Do* 和 *does* **偶尔**用于陈述句中以表示强调。
- **b.** 当**不需要特别强调**时，不要使用 *do / does*。

| ✅ EMPHASIS                                                                             | ❌ NO EMPHASIS NEEDED                                                                              |
| --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Whether**they do in fact** indicate the existence of UFOs is still an open issue. | These drugs may not always be effective.**In fact, they often have** undesired side effects. |

---

### 1.11 全文保持一致性（ensuring consistency throughout a manuscript）

- **a.** 同一个词用**相同格式**。
- **b.** 用**相同语法形式**（并列结构要平行）。
- **c.** 用**相同拼写**（American vs. British，选一种，全文统一）。

| ✅ YES                                                                                                                                     | ❌ NO                                                                                                                |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| In**Phase 1** of the project…, whereas in **Phase 4** we will…                                                               | In the**first phase** of the project…, whereas in **Phase IV** we will…                                |
| This research has three aims: (1)**to increase** efficiency (2) **to enhance** existing features (3) **to lower** costs. | (1)**to increase** efficiency (2) **the enhancement of** existing features (3) **lowering** costs. |
| The**behavior** of the children… the **behavior** of their parents…                                                          | The**behavior** of the children… the **behaviour** of their parents…                                   |

---

### 1.12 翻译本国特有概念（translating concepts that only exist in your country/language）

避免**直译**本国特有的概念（政治实体、传统、节日名称等）。应该：

- **a.** 用你的语言（斜体）+ 英文解释。
- **b.** 直接用英文解释，不用原语言。

| ✅ YOUR LANGUAGE + ENGLISH                                                                                                                 | ✅ ENGLISH                                                                       | ❌ NO                                                                  |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Such preferences are due to the Chinese concept of*yì tóu* **which is related to ideas regarding premonition and superstition**. | The energy plan was approved by the**regional administration in Tuscany**. | Such preferences are due to the Chinese concept of*yì tóu*. Thus… |

---

### 1.13 始终使用相同的关键词（repetition of key words is not a problem）

- **a.** **永远不要为关键词创造同义词**——读者可能认为每个术语含义不同。
- **b.** 重复使用相同的介词不被认为是写作风格差。
- **c.** 当你引用前文的关键词时，**重复该关键词**，而非用泛指代词（如 *this gas, this metal*）替代。

| ✅ YES                                                                                                             | ❌ NO                                                                                                                 |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| In the**first phase** of the project…, whereas in the **fifth phase** we will…                       | In the**first phase** of the project…, whereas in the **fifth stage** we will…                          |
| The**operator** of the PC does x followed by y. Finally, the **operator** does z.                      | The**operator** of the PC does x followed by y. Finally, the **user** does z.                             |
| The solubility of elemental**mercury** is low… However, **mercury** can undergo many transformations. | The solubility of elemental**mercury** is low… However, **this metal** can undergo many transformations. |

💡 **类比理解：** 关键词就像代码中的变量名——你定义了 `userID`，就要一直叫它 `userID`，不能一会儿叫 `userId`，一会儿叫 `accountNumber`，否则读者（编译器）会以为它们是不同的东西。

---

### 1.14 避免 the former / the latter / which 造成歧义

- **a.** 避免使用 *the former, the latter, the first, the second*——**直接重复词语**，节省读者回溯的时间。
- **b.** 如果使用关系从句会导致句子过长，**重复关键词**而非使用 *which*。

| ✅ YES                                                                                                                             | ❌ NO                                                                                                                                       |
| ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| …**Australia**, New Zealand and Canada. From an analysis… **Australia** has been the target of most investigations… | …**Australia**, New Zealand and Canada. From an analysis… **the former country** has been the target of most investigations… |
| …innovative research is**carried out**. **These institutes** are located in various parts of Italy…                  | …innovative research is carried out and**which** are located in various parts of Italy…                                             |

---

### 1.15 避免使用 as / in accordance with / according to 造成歧义

- **a.** 确保清楚表明某事是**符合**还是**不符合**法规和建议。

| ✅ YES                                                                                                                                                                                             | ❌ NO                                                                                                           |
| -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| In 16.6% of the food packages, no country of origin was reported.**This is in direct contrast to** European regulations, which explicitly state that the country of origin must be declared. | In 16.6% of the food packages, no country of origin was reported,**as required by** European regulations. |

---

### 1.16 用否定词表达否定概念（when expressing a negative concept using a negation）

- **a.** 英语倾向于**用否定词直接表达否定概念**，这有助于读者立刻理解负面含义。

| MORE COMMON / LESS FORMAL                                                    | LESS COMMON / MORE FORMAL                                      |
| ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| There are**not as many** opportunities for women as there are for men. | There are**fewer** opportunities for women than for men. |

- **b.** 一个句子中**只需要一个否定词**（双重否定是错误英语）。

| ✅ YES                                                                                                           | ❌ NO                                                                                                              |
| ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| The device was**not** designed to be connected to a network, **either** wired **or** wireless. | The device was**not** designed to be connected to a network, **neither** wired **nor** wireless. |

## Part 2 — Word Order（词序）

### 2.1 主语放在动词前，且尽量靠近句首

主语通常包含**最重要的信息**，应放在句子开头。

| ✅ YES                                                            | ❌ NO                                                            |
| ----------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Several techniques** can be used to address this problem. | To address this problem**several techniques** can be used. |

### 2.2 决定句子最先写什么（decide what to put first）

- **a.** 把**最重要的信息**放在前面，使写作更直接、更令人印象深刻。

| ✅ YES                                        | ❌ NO                                                         |
| --------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **A new cure** was discovered recently. | It was only recently that**a new cure** was discovered. |

### 2.3 不要延迟主语（do not delay the subject）

- **a.** 在提及时间、方式、地点、原因之前，**先写主语**。
- **b.** 如果用了很多连接词（*in particular, generally speaking, consequently, in addition* 等），不要总把它们放在句首。尽量找一个短词（*thus, so, also*）插在**动词之前**。
- **c.** 避免在句首使用非人称 *it*，改用情态动词（*might, need, should*）或副词。
- **d.** 避免用含有 *to be* 的时间段开头句子。

| ✅ YES                                                                                    | ❌ NOT RECOMMENDED                                                                |
| ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| The**samples** were dried after they had spent five minutes in an aqueous solution. | After five minutes in an aqueous solution…, the**samples** were dried.     |
| **The old system** should **thus** not be used.                               | **For this reason**, it is not a good idea to use **the old system**. |
| This**can** be done with the new system.                                            | **It is possible** to do this with the new system.                          |
| We have been studying this problem**for three years**.                              | **They are three years** that we study this problem.                        |

### 2.4 避免过长的主语拖延主要动词（avoid long subjects that delay the main verb）

三种解决方法：

- **a.** 用**主动语态**代替被动语态。
- **b.** 将动词移到**句子开头**。
- **c.** 将长句**拆分为两个短句**。

| ✅ YES                                                                                                                                                                      | ❌ NOT RECOMMENDED                                                                                                                                                                  |
| --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ABC generally**employs** people with a high rate of intelligence, a proven talent for problem-solving, a passion for computers, along with good communication skills. | People with a high rate of intelligence, a proven talent for problem-solving, a passion for computers, along with good communication skills**are generally employed** by ABC. |

💡 **类比理解：** 长主语延迟动词就像一个人说话总喜欢先铺垫很久才说重点——"在我仔细考虑了所有因素、权衡了利弊之后……我同意。"读者在等动词的过程中已经迷失了。

## Part 3 — Acronyms（缩略词）

### 3.1 主要用法（main usage）

- **a.** **第一次**使用缩略词时，先写**全称**，再在括号里写缩略词。之后的行文中直接使用缩略词。
- **b.** 缩略词的每个字母通常**大写**。
- **c.** 不要在缩略词后面**重复其最后一个词**（如 "GUI interface" 中 interface 已经包含在 GUI 的 I 里了）。

| ✅ YES                                                                  | ❌ NO                                                                                    |
| ----------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Orders are dealt with on a**first in first out (FIFO)** basis.    | Orders are dealt with on a**FIFO (first in first out)** basis.                     |
| We are part of a**NASA** project.                                 | We are part of a**Nasa** project.                                                  |
| The**GUI** is user friendly. It does not require a **PIN**. | The**GUI interface** is user friendly. It does not require a **PIN number**. |

### 3.2 语法（grammar）

缩略词和所有名词一样，遵循普通语法规则：

- **a.** 如果缩略词指代**可数实体**，单数时需要**冠词**。
- **b.** 缩略词的**复数**形式通过加 *s* 构成。

| ✅ YES                                   | ❌ NO                                   |
| ---------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| We used**a PC**.                   | We used**PC**.                    |
| Four**PCs** in series were needed. | Four**PC** in series were needed. |

## Part 4 — Capitalization（大写）

### 4.1 标题和章节标题（titles and section headings）

具体格式取决于期刊的风格要求，以下为一般规范：

- **a.** 文章**主标题**中所有词首字母大写，**除了**以下词（除非它们是第一个词）：

  - *a* 和 *the*
  - *it*
  - *and*
  - 所有介词（*by, from, of* 等）
- **b.** 也可以只将**第一个词首字母大写**，其余小写。**章节标题**通常遵循这种格式。
- **c.** 标题末尾**不加句号**（.）。

| ✅ YES                                                               | ❌ NO                                                           |
| -------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **A Guide to the Use of English in Scientific Documents**      | **A Guide To The Use Of English In Scientific Documents** |
| A guide to the use of English in scientific documents*(no period)* | A guide to the use of English in scientific documents**.**      |

### 4.2 日期、国家、国籍、方向词（days, months, countries, nationalities, directions）

- **a.** *north(ern), south(ern), east(ern), west(ern)* 只有在指**地图上正式标注的区域名**时才首字母大写。

  - ✅ **North Korea**, **East Timor**（正式国名）
  - ❌ **South Japan**, **East India**（非正式区域，不是国名）
- **b.** 指代一个国家的**地理区域**时，两种形式均可，且均不大写：

  - ✅ **southern France** / **the south of France**
- **c.** 以下通常首字母大写：*the West, the Middle East, the Far East*。

  - *the northern/southern hemisphere* 可大写可不大写。

| ✅ YES                                       | ❌ NO                                        |
| -------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| in the**south** of France              | in the**South** of France              |
| the**West** and the **Far East** | the**west** and the **far east** |

### 4.3 学术职称、学位、学科、院系（academic titles, degrees, subjects, departments）

- **a.** 职位名称在**正式文件**（简历、会议简介）中通常首字母大写，尤其是该职位**只由一人担任**时（此时不需要 *a/an*）。如果多人担任该职位（需要 *a/an*），则首字母大写不是必须的。

| INITIAL UPPER CASE                                             | INITIAL LOWER CASE                                           |
| -------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| She is now**Associate Professor** at Nanjing University. | He is an**associate professor** at Nanjing University. |

### 4.4 图、表、章节等编号词（figure, table, section etc. with numbers）

- **a.** 当提到带编号的章节、图、表、附录、步骤、阶段等时，**首字母大写**。
- **b.** 没有编号时，**不大写**。

| ✅ YES                                         | ❌ NOT RECOMMENDED                              |
| ---------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| See**Section 2** for further details.    | See the**section 2** for further details. |
| See the**appendix** for further details. | See the**Appendix** for further details.  |

---

## Part 5 — Figures and Tables（图表）

### 5.1 提及图表时的用语（figures, tables）

- **a.** 引用或介绍图表时，**保持简洁**。

| ✅ YES                                               | ❌ NOT RECOMMENDED                                                                                 |
| ---------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Figure 2 below shows** the initial settings. | The following figure (Figure 2)**gives a schematic overview of** the initial settings.       |
| **Figure 3 shows** the architecture.           | The**snapshot depicted in** Figure 3 **shows a view of** the architecture.             |
| For**details, see** (Kyun, 2013).              | For**further details on this topic, the reader is kindly invited to refer to** (Kyun, 2013). |

- **b.** 用 **as** 而非 *as it*。

| ✅ YES                                         | ❌ NO                                             |
| ---------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| **As** can be seen in the figure below… | **As it** can be seen in the figure below… |

### 5.2 图表说明（legends）

图表说明没有统一标准，以下是一种常用格式：

> **Figure 1. The main characteristics of the shock absorbers.**

具体格式要求：

- *Figure / Table / Appendix* 等词首字母**大写**，不使用缩写
- 编号后加**句号（.）**
- 说明文字**首词首字母大写**
- 说明文字末尾加**句号（.）**

## 全课总结

```
Academic Writing Style 五大主题框架：

1. Sentence（句子）        2. Word Order（词序）
   ├─ 每句≤25词              ├─ 主语靠前
   ├─ 时间顺序写              ├─ 最重要信息放首位
   ├─ 避免插入语              ├─ 不延迟主语
   ├─ 动词>名词               └─ 避免长主语拖延动词
   ├─ 形容词>名词
   ├─ 小心代词             3. Acronyms（缩略词）
   ├─ 减少we/us/our           ├─ 首次出现写全称+（缩略词）
   ├─ 避免口语词/缩写          ├─ 每字母大写
   ├─ 全文保持一致            └─ 不重复最后缩写词
   ├─ 重复关键词不是问题
   └─ 用否定词表否定       4. Capitalization（大写）
                              ├─ 标题：内容词大写，虚词不大写
                              ├─ 方向词：只有正式地名才大写
                              ├─ 职称：正式文件/唯一职位大写
                              └─ 编号词（Section 2）大写
                        
                          5. Figures & Tables（图表）
                              ├─ 引用时用简洁语言（shows/see）
                              ├─ 用as不用as it
                              └─ 说明格式：Figure 1. 首词大写. 末尾句号.
```

| 核心口诀             | 记忆要点                           |
| -------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| **短句清晰**   | 每句≤25词，一个信息，时间顺序     |
| **动词优先**   | 动词>名词，形容词>名词，主动>被动  |
| **主语靠前**   | 主语先行，重要信息放首位，不延迟   |
| **统一一致**   | 格式、语法形式、拼写全文统一       |
| **关键词重复** | 不造同义词，不用 the former/latter |
| **缩略词规范** | 首次全称，字母全大写，不重复       |
| **大写有依据** | 正式名称大写，普通描述不大写       |
| **图表从简**   | shows/see 足够，避免冗余表达       |
